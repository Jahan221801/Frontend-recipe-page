Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

•    Total: Approximately 10 minutes


•    Preparation: 5 minutes


•    Cooking: 5 minutes


Ingredients

•    2-3 large eggs
•     Salt, to taste
•     Pepper, to taste
•    1 tablespoon of butter or oil
•    Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

1.     Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until well mixed. Add water or milk for a fluffier texture.

2.    Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.

3.     Cook the omelette: When the butter melts, pour in the eggs. Tilt the pan to spread the eggs evenly.

4.     Add fillings (optional): Once edges set but the center is slightly runny, sprinkle your fillings over half the omelette.

5.     Fold and serve: Fold one edge over the fillings and cook for another minute. Slide onto a plate.

6.     Enjoy: Serve hot, season as needed.
